Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

PROVE IT ("the App") is developed by Auric Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the terms described herein.

Data We Collect

  • Onboarding preferences (name, religion, goals) — stored locally on your device
  • Mission completion data, aura points, streaks, and stats — stored locally
  • Addiction tracking timers and abstinence data — stored locally only
  • App usage analytics (anonymous, aggregated) — via Firebase Analytics
  • Crash reports and performance data — via Firebase Crashlytics

Advertising (AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data including device identifiers, usage data, and location data to serve personalized or contextual ads. You may opt out through your device settings. See Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App. This data is anonymized and used solely to improve the App experience. We do not sell analytics data to third parties.

Local Storage

All personal progress data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not transmit this data to our servers. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local data.

Children's Privacy

PROVE IT is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
